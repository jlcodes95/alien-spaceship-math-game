Start Game Game Rules

Use the arrow keys to move your cannon. To fire at the spaceships, press SPACEBAR to shoot at the spaceship.
When you shoot the correct spaceship with your cannon, you will earn a hit score of 1.
When you shoot an incorrect spaceship with your cannon, your miss score will increase.
When the timer runs out or when any of the spaceship touches the platform or the cannon, the game is over.
